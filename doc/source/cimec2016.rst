CIMeC hands-on methods course, part 1 (6-29 April 2016)
=======================================================

*to be presented by Nick Oosterhof and James V. Haxby*

*Note: This page describes the first part of the course, between 6 and 29 April 2016. The second part of the course, between 4 May and 11 May 2016, is taught by Christoph Braun and will cover analysis of MEEG data. Information about the second part is not presented here.*

Earlier presentations:

- 2016  :ref:`Royal Holloway University of London CoSMoMVPA workshop <rhul2016>`.
- 2014  :ref:`CIMeC 2014 Advanced Neural Decoding <cimec2014>`.
- 2013  (`CoSMo 2013 workshop`_), :ref:`earlier versions of some exercises <cosmo2013>` were presented at the *2013 Summer School in Computational Sensory-Motor Neuroscience*
